About
The Adelphi University Innovation Center is the nexus for inspiring, supporting, and showcasing innovative and interdisciplinary activities within the university and in cooperation with industry and community partners.

As part of our mission, we are proud to share a collection of accessible, web-based applications and to release their source code freely as open-source contributions for the benefit of the software development community.
